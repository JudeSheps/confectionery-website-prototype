Coco's Chocolate

Coco's chocolates are painstakingly crafted with love from the finest ingredients available. Each and every chocolate is handcrafted to perfection. There is a wide variety of chocolates to choose from, including organic chocolates and chocolates that are gluten, nut and dairy-free.

About

Coco Velvet became obsessed with creating the perfect chocolate when she was in school for the culinary arts. For the past decade, she has delighted the palates of chocolate lovers from all over the world.
